📘 Understanding EMI and how this tool works

What is EMI? Equated Monthly Installment – a fixed amount you pay each month to repay a loan. It comprises principal and interest. Our AI EMI calculator uses the reducing balance method (standard formula):

EMI = [P × r × (1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n – 1]

Where P = principal, r = monthly interest rate, n = months. All calculations happen instantly in your browser – no server, no data leak.

Reducing balance vs flat rate

Reducing balance applies interest only on the outstanding principal, which is fairer and standard for home/car loans. Flat rate loans charge interest on the full principal throughout tenure – our calculator uses reducing balance, the norm for banks.

Tips to reduce EMI burden

  • Make prepayments (use the prepayment field) – reduces principal & total interest.
  • Choose shorter tenure if you can afford higher EMI.
  • Compare interest rates across lenders.
